02 Typography

Font Family

We use Roboto (300, 400, 700) for body text and Montserrat (700) for our display headings. In Our Hero-Section we use Oswald (700). You'll find them all on Google Fonts.

We use Font Awesome as our iconfont. You can download the whole kit on the Font Awesome Website.
